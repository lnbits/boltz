Boltz is a privacy-focused, non-custodial exchange service that lets you convert between Lightning and onchain Bitcoin. Unlike centralized exchanges, Boltz never holds your funds - you stay in complete control throughout the entire swap process.

What This Extension Does

This extension connects your LNbits wallet to Boltz, making it easy to convert between Lightning and onchain Bitcoin in three ways:

Onchain to Lightning (Swap IN)

Convert your onchain Bitcoin to Lightning funds for instant payments. You send Bitcoin to an address and receive Lightning in your wallet.

Lightning to Onchain (Swap OUT)

Send Lightning funds to any Bitcoin address. Perfect for withdrawing to cold storage, exchanges, or services that don't support Lightning yet.

Auto Sweep to Onchain (Automatic Swap OUT)

Set up automatic conversions from Lightning to onchain when your balance reaches a certain amount. Great for regular sweeps to onchain without manual intervention.

{% raw %} Every swap consists of 2 onchain transactions, lockup and claim / refund, routing fees and a Boltz fee of {{ boltzConfig.fee_percentage }}%. {% endraw %}

Fee Example: Lightning to Onchain

{% raw %} You want to swap out {{ boltzExample.amount }} sats, Lightning to Onchain:

  • Onchain lockup tx fee: ~{{ boltzExample.onchain_boltz }} sats
  • Onchain claim tx fee: {{ boltzExample.onchain_lnbits }} sats (hardcoded)
  • Routing fees (paid by you): unknown
  • Boltz fees: {{ boltzExample.boltz_fee }} sats ({{ boltzConfig.fee_percentage }}%)
  • Fees total: {{ boltzExample.reverse_fee_total }} sats + routing fees
  • You receive: {{ boltzExample.reverse_receive }} sats

onchain_amount_received = amount - (amount * boltz_fee / 100) - lockup_fee - claim_fee

Fee Example: Onchain to Lightning

You want to swap in {{ boltzExample.amount }} sats, Onchain to Lightning:

  • Onchain lockup tx fee: whatever you choose when paying
  • Onchain claim tx fee: ~{{ boltzExample.onchain_boltz }} sats
  • Routing fees (paid by boltz): unknown
  • Boltz fees: {{ boltzExample.boltz_fee }} sats ({{ boltzConfig.fee_percentage }}%)
  • Fees total: {{ boltzExample.normal_fee_total }} sats + lockup_fee
  • You pay onchain: {{ boltzExample.normal_expected_amount }} sats + lockup_fee
  • You receive lightning: {{ boltzExample.amount }} sats

onchain_payment = amount + (amount * boltz_fee / 100) + claim_fee

Common Questions

What happens if a swap fails?

If a swap fails, your funds are automatically refunded to the address you specified. You can also manually trigger a refund if needed.

How long does a swap take?

Swaps typically complete within minutes, depending on network congestion and confirmation times. Lightning to onchain swaps can use "instant settlement" for faster processing.

Is my Bitcoin safe?

Yes! Boltz uses atomic swaps, which means either the swap completes successfully or your funds are returned. Boltz never has custody of your Bitcoin.
